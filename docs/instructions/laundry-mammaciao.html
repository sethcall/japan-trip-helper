Comfort Aoki (Laundromat)

Itinerary Instructions

Location: 17-2, Shiba 3-Chōme, Minato, Tokyo, Japan 105-0014

Distance: Approx. 8 min walk from The Prince Park Tower Tokyo.

Hours: 24 hour coin operated laundromat.

Background Info

This is a standard Japanese coin laundry (coin laundry is often called "coin laundry" in Japan). These facilities are typically clean, safe, and efficient.

Tips for Visitors

  • Coins: Machines typically accept 100 yen coins. There is usually a change machine, but it's good to bring some 100 yen coins just in case.
  • Detergent: Many modern machines automatically add detergent, but check the signs. If not, there's usually a vending machine for small packets.
  • Shoes: Take your shoes off if there is a raised floor area (tatami style), though most modern laundromats don't require this.
  • While you wait: There might be a convenience store nearby to grab a coffee or snack.

Exploring Afterwards

  • Shiba Park: A great place to relax or walk while waiting for your laundry. Google Maps
  • Petit Accueil: A nearby cafe to grab a coffee and wait. Google Maps
  • Tokyo Tower: Just a short walk away if you have a longer wait time. Google Maps
