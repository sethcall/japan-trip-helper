The Prince Park Tower Tokyo

Itinerary Instructions

Call Family: Check-in Dec 20, Check-out Dec 26. Confirmation: CI5KI22R and CI5KI27Y.

Edwards Family: Check-in Dec 22, Check-out Dec 26. Confirmation: CI5KI1EO and CI5KI1H0.

Transfer: Take Taxi from airport (NRT/HND). Airport Limousine Bus is also an option but Taxi is easier.

Location: 4-8-1 Shiba Koen Minato-Ku Tokyo 195-8563, JP

Background Info

It’s about a 12-minute walk (or a free shuttle ride) from The Prince Park Tower Tokyo to the JR Yamanote Line at Hamamatsuchō Station. The vibe is a balance of calm, greenery, and cultural heritage, set against the backdrop of a vibrant, modern city—making it an ideal neighborhood for families who want both relaxation and excitement at their doorstep.

Within the hotel itself, there’s no shortage of dining and amenities. Guests can choose from elegant sushi, teppanyaki, and kaiseki restaurants, a classic steakhouse, and casual lounges with Tokyo Tower views. The hotel also offers a spa, fitness center, and wellness facilities, plus a Sky Lounge bar perfect for parents once the kids are settled.

Tips for Visitors

  • Shuttle Service: Utilize the free shuttle bus to Hamamatsucho Station for easy access to the JR Yamanote Line.
  • Tokyo Tower Views: The hotel is famous for its proximity to Tokyo Tower. The surrounding Shiba Park is great for morning walks.
  • Concierge: Use the concierge to help with taxi arrangements, especially if you need a larger vehicle for the family.

Exploring Afterwards

  • Tokyo Tower: The iconic red and white tower offering panoramic city views. (~3 min walk) Google Maps
  • Zojoji Temple: A historic Buddhist temple standing impressively in front of Tokyo Tower. (~5 min walk) Google Maps
  • Shiba Park: One of Japan's oldest parks, perfect for a relaxing stroll. (~1 min walk) Google Maps
