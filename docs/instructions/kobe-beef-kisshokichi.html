Kobe Beef Kisshokichi

Itinerary Instructions

Date: Dec 27 (Saturday) at 7:00 PM.

Location: Kyoto Main Store, 521 Kamiosakacho, Nakagyo Ward, Kyoto.

Transfer: Taxi or Karasuma Subway Line.

Background Info

Kobe Beef Kisshokichi specializes in A5-grade Kobe Beef, purchasing whole cows to ensure quality and offer various cuts. The Kyoto Main Store offers a dining experience with views (often of the Kamo River/Pontocho area depending on exact seating). They serve Kobe beef in various styles, including steak, sukiyaki, and sushi.

Tips for Visitors

  • Richness: Kobe beef is extremely marbled and rich. You don't need a huge steak to feel satisfied; the quality is about the texture and flavor.
  • Cooking: If having steak, medium-rare is usually recommended to let the fat melt perfectly.
  • Certificates: They often display the certificates of the cows they purchased—a sign of authenticity.

Exploring Afterwards

  • Pontocho Alley: An atmospheric narrow alley packed with restaurants and bars. (~0 min walk) Google Maps
  • Kamo River: A beautiful river perfect for an evening stroll along the banks. (~1 min walk) Google Maps
  • Nishiki Market: Known as "Kyoto's Kitchen," a lively market for food lovers. (~12 min walk) Google Maps
