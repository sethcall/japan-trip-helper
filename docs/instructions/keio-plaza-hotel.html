Keio Plaza Hotel Tokyo

Itinerary Instructions

Check-in: Dec 29. Check-out: Jan 1.

Call Family Confirmation: 27044SF290386.

Edwards Family Room: Deluxe Plus Twin and Triple + Standard Plus King.

Transfer: From Tokyo Station, take JR Chuo Line (Rapid) to Shinjuku Station, then Walk or Taxi.

Location: 2-2-1 Nishi-Shinjuku Shinjuku-Ku Tokio 160-8330, JP.

Background Info

Keio Plaza Hotel is a massive, iconic hotel in the Nishi-Shinjuku skyscraper district. It was the first high-rise hotel in Japan. It is known for its excellent service, vast array of restaurants, and convenient location near Shinjuku Station (the world's busiest train station).

Tips for Visitors

  • Shinjuku Station: It is huge and confusing. If walking, take the "West Exit" and use the underground moving walkway or the surface level streets. If taking a taxi, it's a short ride from the station.
  • Airport Limo: The hotel is a major hub for the Airport Limousine Bus, which is a very convenient way to get to Haneda/Narita.
  • Convenience: There is a convenience store inside the hotel (often on the lobby or basement level).
