Best of Shibuya Food Tour

Itinerary Instructions

Date: Dec 30 (Tuesday) at 4:00 PM.

Meeting Point: Wall Mural of Hachiko (NOT the statue itself, but the mural near the station exit).

Confirmation: PE150420017.

Background Info

Shibuya is a food lover's paradise, but it can be overwhelming. This tour likely takes you to hidden gems, izakayas, and standing bars that you might miss on your own. It's a great way to try a variety of dishes like kushikatsu, sushi, or yakitori while soaking in the neon-lit atmosphere.

Tips for Visitors

  • Meeting Point: The "Hachiko" area is extremely crowded. Be very specific about looking for the "Wall Mural" and arrive early to find your guide holding a sign.
  • Appetite: Come hungry! Pace yourself.
  • Comfort: You will be walking and standing a lot.

Exploring Afterwards

  • Mega Don Quijote Shibuya: A massive discount store perfect for souvenirs and snacks. (~5 min walk) Google Maps
  • Shibuya Center-gai: A vibrant street packed with fashion, music, and youth culture. (~2 min walk) Google Maps
  • Shibuya Parco: Home to the Nintendo Store, Pokemon Center, and cool fashion brands. (~10 min walk) Google Maps
