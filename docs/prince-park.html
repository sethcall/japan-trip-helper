Prince Park Tower Tips

Our Location: Shiba Park

Our hotel in Tokyo is located in Shiba Koen (or 'Shiba Park'). It is one of the oldest parks in Japan and offers a beautiful green escape right in the middle of the city.

The park is famous for its proximity to Tokyo Tower and Zojoji Temple, providing a unique mix of traditional and modern Tokyo landmarks.
