Background Info

TeamLab Borderless is a world of artworks without boundaries, a museum without a map created by the art collective teamLab. Works move out of rooms, communicate with other works, influence, and sometimes intermingle with each other with no boundaries. It recently reopened at a new location in Azabudai Hills.

Unlike TeamLab Planets, which has water features and requires being barefoot, Borderless is a dry museum where you explore freely. It is the world's most visited digital art museum.

Tips for Visitors

  • App: Download the TeamLab app to interact with some exhibits and get explanations (there are no placards).
  • Clothing: Wear comfortable shoes (lots of walking). Avoid short skirts or baggy shorts because of mirrored floors. White/plain clothes look best in photos as projections show up on you.
  • Lockers: Use the free lockers for coats and bags. Travel light inside.
  • En Tea House: Don't miss the tea house inside where flowers bloom in your tea cup.
  • Sketch Ocean: Great for kids (and adults) to draw sea creatures that come to life on the walls.

Exploring Afterwards
