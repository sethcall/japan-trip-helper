Background Info

The Thousand Kyoto is a modern luxury hotel located just steps from Kyoto Station. Its design concept is "Comfort Minimalism," offering a serene escape from the bustling city. It blends traditional Kyoto aesthetics with modern architecture. The name "Thousand" reflects the millennium of wisdom and hospitality of Kyoto.

Tips for Visitors

  • Location: Its proximity to Kyoto Station makes it an incredible base for day trips (like Nara or Fushimi Inari) and using the Shinkansen.
  • Concierge: Known for excellent service; use them for last-minute restaurant bookings or taxi arrangements.
  • Green Tea: The hotel emphasizes tea culture; enjoy their high-quality local tea selection.

Exploring Afterwards

  • Kyoto Tower: Enjoy panoramic views of the city from the observation deck right opposite the hotel. (~2 min walk) Google Maps
  • Higashi Honganji Temple: A massive wooden temple hall just a short walk away. (~7 min walk) Google Maps
  • Kyoto Station Skyway: A free aerial walkway within the station offering great views of the tower. (~5 min walk) Google Maps
