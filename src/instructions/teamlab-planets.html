TeamLab Planets

Itinerary Instructions

Date: Dec 30 (Tuesday) at 9:00 AM.

Transfer: Take Taxi from Keio Plaza (easiest). Or Train (requires transfers).

Location: Toyosu 6-1-16 Toyosu, Koto-ku, Tokyo.

Background Info

TeamLab Planets is a museum where you walk through water. It consists of four massive exhibition spaces and two gardens. It is a "Body Immersive" concept where the boundaries between the body and the artwork dissolve. You enter barefoot.

Tips for Visitors

  • Clothing: You WILL walk through knee-deep water. Wear pants that roll up easily (or shorts). Avoid tights/pantyhose. Skirts are risky due to mirror floors (wear shorts underneath).
  • Shoes: You must remove shoes and socks at the entrance. Lockers are provided.
  • Towels: They provide towels to dry your feet after the water sections.
  • Wetness: You won't get soaked (unless you fall!), but your feet and calves will get wet.

Exploring Afterwards

  • Urban Dock LaLaport Toyosu: A large seaside mall with shops, restaurants, and a cinema. Google Maps
  • Toyosu Market: Visit the famous wholesale fish market that replaced Tsukiji. Google Maps
  • Toyosu Gururi Park: A waterfront park offering beautiful views of the Tokyo skyline and Rainbow Bridge. Google Maps
