TeamLab Biovortex Kyoto

Itinerary Instructions

Date: Dec 28 (Sunday) at 4:00 PM.

Transfer: 7-10 minute walk from JR Kyoto Station.

Confirmation: KYFV4HCX7Y4WN-0001.

Background Info

Opened in late 2025 (check current status as this is a new future-dated venue in the itinerary), TeamLab Biovortex is a new permanent exhibition in Kyoto. It explores themes of life, death, and the universe through complex, interactive digital art. Unlike the "flat" feeling of some projections, this venue aims for a more organic, 3D, and immersive "vortex" of life.

Tips for Visitors

  • New Venue: Since this is a new location, it will likely be very popular. Stick to your timed entry.
  • Comfort: As with all TeamLab venues, wear flat, comfortable shoes.
  • Mirrors: Avoid short skirts as floors may be mirrored.
  • App: Have the TeamLab app ready.
