Location Date (Optional) Description
Activity: Senso-ji Temple & Nakamise St Google Maps Dec 24 Enter through Kaminarimon Gate, walk down Nakamise Street for snacks and souvenirs, and explore the historic Senso-ji temple grounds.
Restaurant: Sushiro Asakusa Azumabashi Google Maps Dec 24 (Lunch) Popular conveyor-belt sushi restaurant located just a short walk from Asakusa Station. Great for a casual, fun lunch.
Activity: Marunouchi Illumination Google Maps Dec 24 (Evening) Champagne gold lights illuminating the entire boulevard. Located immediately from JR/Tokyo Metro "Tokyo Station" Marunouchi South Exit.
Arcade: Taito Station Akihabara Google Maps Dec 21/22 (Evening) Akihabara is renowned for its multi-floor arcades. Taito Station is one of the most recognizable, offering everything from retro games to UFO catchers.
Activity: Harajuku & Omotesando Google Maps Dec 25 Stroll through Takeshita Street for colorful shops and crepes, walk Cat Street, and explore the beautiful Omotesando illuminations.
Activity: Gion & Higashiyama Night Stroll Google Maps Dec 26 (Night) A perfect after-dinner activity. Walk through Hanamikoji Street and along the Shirakawa Canal, ending at Yasaka Shrine.
Activity: Arashiyama Morning Google Maps Dec 28 Start at Otagi Nenbutsu-ji and walk downhill through the preserved streets to the famous Bamboo Grove.
Activity: Nishiki Market Google Maps Dec 29 Explore "Kyoto's Kitchen", a narrow shopping street lined with more than one hundred shops and restaurants.
Activity: Ginza Shopping Google Maps Dec 31 Enjoy high-end shopping and dining in Ginza. Stores typically close around 6:00 PM on New Year's Eve.
Activity: Oji Inari-Jinja Shrine Fox Parade Google Maps Dec 31 (Midnight) A unique New Year's Eve tradition where locals dress as foxes and parade to the shrine.
