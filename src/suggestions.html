Location Description
Arcade: Taito Station Akihabara Google Maps Akihabara is renowned for its multi-floor arcades (Game Centers). These venues offer everything from classic retro games to the latest rhythm and crane games (UFO catchers), forming a core part of the district's otaku culture. Taito Station is one of the most recognizable, easily identified by its giant Space Invader logo.
