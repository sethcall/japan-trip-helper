The Sunday Strategy

Shiba Park Morning & Ginza Afternoon

Suggested Schedule

07:00 - 08:30 Walk Shiba Park, Zojoji Temple, and climb Atago Shrine stairs.
08:30 - 09:30 Breakfast at Le Pain Quotidien (Shiba Park).
10:00 - 10:30 Transit to Ginza (Taxi recommended for ease).
10:30 - 12:30 Shop at Itoya (Stationery) and Marronnier Gate (Standard Products/Uniqlo).
12:30 Lunch in Ginza (plenty of options) + Mont Blanc for dessert.
Afternoon Walk the 'Pedestrian Paradise' on Ginza main street.

Sunday Locations

Click on any location name to see the address card.

Location Description
Temple: Zojoji Temple Google Maps The massive temple grounds are accessible early. Don't miss the Sangedatsumon Gate and Tokyo Tower view.
Shrine: Atago Shrine Google Maps Famous for the steep 'Stone Steps of Success'. A serene power spot atop a hill.
Breakfast/Cafe: Le Pain Quotidien (Shiba Park) Google Maps Located directly in Shiba Park. Excellent coffee and bread baskets. Family-friendly.
Shopping: Standard Products (Ginza) Google Maps Minimalist, high-quality household goods. Located in Marronnier Gate Ginza 2 (6th Floor).
Shopping: Uniqlo Tokyo (Flagship) Google Maps Global flagship store with custom t-shirt printing and flower shops inside.
Stationery: G. Itoya (Ginza) Google Maps A 12-story stationery store that feels like a museum. Opens at 10:00 AM on Sundays.
Dessert/Cafe: Ginza Miyuki-kan Google Maps Legendary Mont Blanc cake (chestnut cream). Retro-European style kissaten atmosphere.
Department Store: Ginza Mitsukoshi (Depachika) Google Maps Explore the basement floors (Depachika) for art-like cakes, wagashi, and fruits.
Shopping: Don Quijote Roppongi Google Maps The chaos option. Sells everything from KitKats to luxury watches in a jungle-like display.
