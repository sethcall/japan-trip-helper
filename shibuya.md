Walk & Shop option - Cat Street

Cat Street stretches for about one kilometer and is easily accessible from several major train stations. Here's how to get there:

From Shibuya Station: Take the Hachiko Exit and walk towards the Shibuya Modi department store. Cat Street starts just past the tower, on the left side of Meiji-dori Avenue. The walk takes about 5-8 minutes.  Shibuya Modi Google Map Address: https://maps.app.goo.gl/51631xRdruxkBMvg9

From Harajuku Station: Take the Omotesando Exit and walk down the main Omotesando avenue. Turn right at the Ralph Lauren store, and Cat Street will be on your right after a short walk. It takes about 8-10 minutes.  Ralph Lauren Location on Google Maps: https://maps.app.goo.gl/wgQqb2zUrvNhKDRv9
From Meiji-jingumae 'Harajuku' Station: This is the closest station on the Harajuku side. Take Exit 4 or 7, and it's about a 5-minute walk.
From Omotesando Station: Take Exit A1. Walk down Omotesando avenue towards Harajuku and turn left at the corner with the large Gyre shopping center. Cat Street is just a short walk from there, taking about 6-8 minutes.
