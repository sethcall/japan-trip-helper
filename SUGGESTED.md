## Suggestion Details

<a id="asakusa"></a>
### Senso-ji Temple & Nakamise St
- **Type:** Activity
- **Date:** Dec 24
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Senso-ji+Temple+Asakusa+Tokyo)
- **Description:** Enter through Kaminarimon Gate, walk down Nakamise Street for snacks and souvenirs, and explore the historic Senso-ji temple grounds.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/senso-ji.html)

<a id="sushiro"></a>
### Sushiro Asakusa Azumabashi
- **Type:** Restaurant
- **Date:** Dec 24 (Lunch)
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Sushiro+Asakusa+Azumabashi)
- **Description:** Popular conveyor-belt sushi restaurant located just a short walk from Asakusa Station. Great for a casual, fun lunch.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/sushiro-asakusa.html)

<a id="marunouchi"></a>
### Marunouchi Illumination
- **Type:** Activity
- **Date:** Dec 24 (Evening)
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Marunouchi+Naka-dori+Tokyo)
- **Description:** Champagne gold lights illuminating the entire boulevard. Located immediately from JR/Tokyo Metro "Tokyo Station" Marunouchi South Exit.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/marunouchi-illumination.html)

<a id="akihabara"></a>
### Taito Station Akihabara
- **Type:** Arcade
- **Date:** Dec 21/22 (Evening)
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Taito+Station+Akihabara+4-2-2+Sotokanda,+Chiyoda-ku,+Tokyo)
- **Description:** Akihabara is renowned for its multi-floor arcades. Taito Station is one of the most recognizable, offering everything from retro games to UFO catchers.
  
  ![Akihabara Arcade](src/assets/photos/akihabara.jpg)
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/taito-station-akihabara.html)

### Harajuku & Omotesando
- **Type:** Activity
- **Date:** Dec 25
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Takeshita+Street+Harajuku+Tokyo)
- **Description:** Stroll through Takeshita Street for colorful shops and crepes, walk Cat Street, and explore the beautiful Omotesando illuminations.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/takeshita-street.html)

### Gion & Higashiyama Night Stroll
- **Type:** Activity
- **Date:** Dec 26 (Night)
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gion+Kyoto)
- **Description:** A perfect after-dinner activity. Walk through Hanamikoji Street and along the Shirakawa Canal, ending at Yasaka Shrine.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/yasaka-shrine.html)

### Arashiyama Morning
- **Type:** Activity
- **Date:** Dec 28
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Arashiyama+Bamboo+Grove+Kyoto)
- **Description:** Start at Otagi Nenbutsu-ji and walk downhill through the preserved streets to the famous Bamboo Grove.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/arashiyama-bamboo.html)

### Nishiki Market
- **Type:** Activity
- **Date:** Dec 29
- **Map:** [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Nishiki+Market+Kyoto)
- **Description:** Explore "Kyoto's Kitchen", a narrow shopping street lined with more than one hundred shops and restaurants.
- **Link:** [View Card](src/cards/nishiki-market.html)
